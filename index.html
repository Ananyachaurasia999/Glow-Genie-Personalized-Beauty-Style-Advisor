✨ GlowMuse DIY Tips ✨


💆‍♀️ How to Identify Your Skin Type
  • Wash your face and wait 1 hour without applying anything.
  • If skin feels tight – it's dry. If it looks shiny – it's oily. Mix = combination.
  • Tip: Use blotting paper – press on T-zone to check oil.
🎨 How to Know Your Skin Tone
  • Check your veins under natural light:
  • Green veins = warm tone, Blue = cool tone, Both = neutral.
  • Tip: Gold jewelry suits warm; Silver suits cool.
👗 How to Know Your Body Shape
  • Measure shoulders, bust, waist, and hips.
  • Hourglass: Balanced bust & hips, smaller waist.
  • Pear: Hips wider than bust. Apple: Broader upper body.
  • Tip: Use string or measuring tape to compare proportions.
💇‍♀️ DIY Hair Porosity Test
  • Drop a clean strand of hair in a glass of water.
  • If it floats: Low porosity – use light oils like argan.
  • If it sinks: High porosity – use heavier oils like castor, shea butter.
  • Tip: Avoid protein-heavy products for high porosity hair.
💄 Basic Makeup Matching Tip
  • For cool undertones: Use pinks, mauves, and cool reds.
  • For warm tones: Use peach, coral, and orange-based shades.
  • Neutral tone: Most shades suit you. Choose based on occasion.
🌿 Desi Skincare Tip
  • Multani mitti + rose water = perfect for oily skin.
  • Raw milk + honey = glow boost for dry skin.
  • Besan + turmeric + curd = tan removal & brightening.

💫 Hair Care Tips


  • Know your Hair Type: Straight, Wavy, Curly, or Coily — different types need different care.
  • Porosity Test: Take one hair strand and drop it in a glass of water - check it whether it floats or shinks
  • Scalp Care: Dry scalp? Try coconut oil massage. Oily scalp? Use aloe vera gel or apple cider vinegar rinse.
  • DIY Hair Pack: Mix curd + aloe vera + 1 vitamin E capsule → Apply for 30 mins before washing.
  • Hair Wash Frequency: Dry hair? 2x a week. Oily hair? 3x a week.
  • Comb tip: Use a wide-tooth wooden comb — reduces breakage.
  • Never apply oil on dirty scalp! Always apply oil one day before washing for deep nourishment.
  • Use silk pillowcases or braid before sleep to avoid breakage.
